Get the Infinity Stones

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four infinity stones below. By clicking on a infinity stone, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your total score to random number. You lose the game if your score is above the random number.
Each time the game starts, the value of each infinity stone changes.
Hurry before Thanos collects all the crystals and wipes out half the universe!
